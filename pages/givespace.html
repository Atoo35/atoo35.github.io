GiveSpace

A social donation platform built on Lens Protocol that brings together donors and NGOs to augment impact through harnessing web3 innovation.

The main aim of this project is to incentivize people to donate to NGOs and also to make the process of donation more transparent and efficient. We tried to gamify the donation process by minting dynamic NFTs for donors which they can potentially upgrade by spending the native token $WTR to then receive say discounts etc from other companies or just build a proof of reputation.


This project was a part of the OxfordHack 2022 which took place in Oxford University along with partnership with Major League Hacking (MLH).
