Alarmclock

This android app was built as a Third Year college project. It is a basic alarm clock but with a twist on how to snooze or cancel the alarm.
When a person is waking up to the alarm he/she cannot open the eyes to the fullest to understand what is happening or where to tap to cancel or snooze the alarm (that happens to me atleast). So we developed the app so that the person just needs to blindly shake the phone to snooze and wave over the phone to cancel the alarm.

This app contains other basic features as stopwatch and timers as well.
A secret thing: Long pressing on the home page will open a small calculator :P.

How to run?

1. Install android studio 2. Download the code. 3. Compile and run.
